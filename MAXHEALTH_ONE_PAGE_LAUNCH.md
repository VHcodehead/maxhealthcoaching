## IF SOMETHING ISN'T WORKING

**Low Views?**
- Re-examine your hook (first 3 seconds)
- Try a different hook formula from Quick Reference
- Post at different time
- Is audio trending? (check if sound is popular on Reels)

**No Saves/Shares?**
- People are watching but not saving
- Hook might be good but content isn't useful
- Add more actionable value or clearer CTA

**No DMs/Link Clicks?**
- Your CTA isn't clear enough
- Make it more explicit ("Link in bio to apply")
- Make urgency more obvious ("2 spots left")

**Still Nothing Working After 5 Posts?**
- You might be over-thinking it
- Just keep posting
- Post #47 works better than post #1
- Data beats intuition—let metrics guide you

## CAUTION: COMMON MISTAKES

DO NOT:
- [ ] Post the same content every day (boring)
- [ ] Ignore comments/DMs (kills momentum)
- [ ] Post without a clear CTA (wasted reach)
- [ ] Post at 9:00 AM or 6:00 PM (everyone does)
- [ ] Make videos too polished (kills authenticity)
- [ ] Generic fitness advice (you need platform/coaching angle)
- [ ] Fake scarcity ("only 5 spots" if you have infinite)

DO:
- [ ] Post consistently (daily Stories, 1-2 Reels/day)
- [ ] Respond fast (2 hours max for DMs)
- [ ] Track everything (spreadsheet is your friend)
- [ ] Adjust based on data (not feelings)
- [ ] Keep it real (phone camera is fine)
- [ ] Specific to your platform/system (that's your edge)
- [ ] Tell the truth about your 20-spot limit (honesty converts)
