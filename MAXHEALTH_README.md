## COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Can I start posting before filming everything?**
A: Yes. Post your first Reel, measure for 3 days, adjust, then post more. Momentum + data beats perfection.

**Q: What if I'm not comfortable being on camera?**
A: These scripts are written to feel authentic and raw, not polished. You'll actually look BETTER on camera being real than trying to be perfect.

**Q: How long will this take?**
A: Filming day = 3-4 hours. Editing = 2 hours for a week of content. Daily posting/engagement = 30-60 minutes per day.

**Q: What if I don't have an "after" photo yet?**
A: Use your current look. The platform value proposition isn't just your body—it's the AI, the personalization, the weekly coaching. Your credibility is in the system, not just transformation photos.

**Q: Should I use hashtags?**
A: Yes. Always 25-30 hashtags per post. Use the strategy from Quick Reference doc.

**Q: What if a video doesn't perform well?**
A: Delete it (optionally) and move on. Post #47 works better than #1. Don't overthink underperformers.
