## HOOK FORMULAS (Copy-Paste Ready)

Use these sentence structures for your first 3 seconds. Adapt to your content:

### Pattern 1: The Shocking Claim
"I wasted [X years/time/money] doing [popular thing]. Here's why it's wrong."
Example: "I wasted 8 years counting calories. Here's why it's wrong."

### Pattern 2: The Direct Contradiction
"[Popular belief]. Actually, [opposite truth]."
Example: "You need 2-hour workouts. Actually, 30 minutes works better."

### Pattern 3: The Question Mark
"[Assumption]? Wrong. Here's what actually works."
Example: "Your genetics are holding you back? Wrong. Here's what is."

### Pattern 4: The "This Could Be You"
"She thought [impossible outcome] was impossible. [Result happened]."
Example: "She thought abs were impossible for her body. Look at her now."

### Pattern 5: The Controversial Take
"Everyone's telling you [mainstream advice]. Nobody's talking about [contrarian truth]."
Example: "Everyone's telling you to eat less. Nobody's talking about eating the right foods."

### Pattern 6: The Visual Shock
[Do something unexpected on camera for 0.5 seconds]
"This changed my [body/life/results]."

### Pattern 7: The Time Compression
"[Result] in [X time]. Here's exactly how."
Example: "4-week transformation. Here's exactly how."

### Pattern 8: The Before/After Hook
[Show before photo]
"I looked like this. Then I [action]. [Show after]."

### Pattern 9: The "Most People Don't Know"
"Most people don't know [fact]. [Fact] happens because [explanation]."
Example: "Most people don't know scale weight is useless. That's because it tracks water, not fat."

### Pattern 10: The Myth Deconstruction
"The fitness industry tells you [myth]. The truth? [Truth]."
Example: "The fitness industry tells you supplements make the difference. The truth? 90% comes from basics."

## MUSIC RECOMMENDATIONS (For Video Edits)

Use trending audio from each platform. Here's how to find winners:

### For Transformation Content
- Search "motivational" or "pump up" on TikTok
- Filter for 1M+ uses (popular = algorithm-friendly)
- Look for trending sounds in Instagram Reels (top right corner shows trending audios)

### For Educational/Myth-Bust
- Use minor-to-major key transitions (drama)
- Pick upbeat but not overbearing
- TikTok audio library: Search "teaching" or "revelation"

### For BTS/Demo Content
- Modern, slightly tech-forward beats
- Not overdone fitness clichies
- "Cinematic" vibe without being too polished

### For Urgency/CTA Content
- Aggressive, motivational beats
- Feels like movie trailer
- Higher tempo = urgency signal

## EDITING CHECKLIST (Before Posting)

Before uploading ANY video, run through:

**Visual Quality:**
- [ ] No blurry frames
- [ ] Good lighting (no harsh shadows)
- [ ] Correct aspect ratio (1080x1920 for Reels/TikTok)
- [ ] No awkward jump cuts
- [ ] Transitions smooth and on-beat

**Audio:**
- [ ] Voice audio clear and not distorted
- [ ] Music cuts off cleanly (no fade ruins momentum)
- [ ] Voiceover synced to visuals
- [ ] No background noise (unless intentional)

**On-Screen Text:**
- [ ] Big enough to read on phone
- [ ] On-screen for minimum 1.5 seconds
- [ ] Appears at key moments (not blocking faces)
- [ ] Font consistent throughout
- [ ] Spelling/grammar checked

**Timing:**
- [ ] Hook is solid 0-3 seconds
- [ ] Pacing matches music beat
- [ ] Total length appropriate (35-60 sec)
- [ ] CTA appears at the end (3-5 seconds)

**Metadata:**
- [ ] Caption written (use templates above)
- [ ] Hashtags added (25-30 total)
- [ ] Thumbnail selected (if custom)
- [ ] Link in bio referenced in caption

"What's the cost of NOT doing this? Another year of mediocre results? My clients typically invest $150-500/month to get $1000+ worth of transformation value. This is an investment in yourself, not an expense."

### "I can just figure this out myself"
"You could. You also could build a house yourself, but hiring a contractor is faster and better. I've spent 10+ years learning what works. My clients get a decade of knowledge compressed into weeks."

### "I've tried coaching before and it didn't work"
"What happened? Usually one of three things: 1) The plan wasn't personalized, 2) There was no accountability, 3) The coach didn't adjust. I solve all three. Let's talk about what actually happened."

### "I don't have time for this"
"Most clients spend 4-5 hours per week on it. How many hours do you spend scrolling? This is less time than that, with a specific outcome."

### "How do I know this actually works?"
"Fair. That's why I put my transformation on my profile. That's why I show client results. And that's why I offer to chat first—no commitment. Let's talk about your specific situation."

### "I'm afraid I'll quit"
"Accountability is literally what you're paying for. The weekly check-in isn't about judgment. It's about momentum. Quitting solo is easy. Quitting when someone's counting on you is harder. That's the point."
