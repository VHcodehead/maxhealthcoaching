# MaxHealth Coaching: Video Script Filming Guide
**Print This & Use It On Set**

---

## FILMING TIPS BEFORE YOU START

### Phone Setup
- Use landscape mode (stronger videos)
- Clean lens before filming
- Good lighting (natural window light = best)
- No notifications showing (do not disturb)
- Tripod or stable surface (no shaky cam)

### Energy & Delivery
- First take is usually best (authenticity over perfection)
- Speak naturally (not like you're reading a script)
- Faster pacing = higher engagement
- Pause between thoughts (creates rhythm)
- Look directly at camera (builds connection)

### Retakes
- If you mess up, just start over
- Don't say "cut" or apologize
- Edit out the bad takes later
- 2-3 good takes per video is plenty
